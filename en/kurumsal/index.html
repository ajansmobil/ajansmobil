Corporate

Ajans Mobil was established in 2018 by Yunus Sevğanen to offer innovative and creative solutions to the digital world. Since our establishment, we have stood out with our customer-focused approach, adaptation to technological innovations and experience in the industry.

Our Mission

As Ajans Mobil, our mission is to help brands show a strong presence in the digital world, bring them together with their target audiences and offer innovative strategies in this process. Our expert team in design, software and social media is with you at every stage of your projects.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading position among digital agencies in Turkey and the world and add permanent values to our customers by signing projects that bring technology together with creativity. In today's world where digital transformation is rapidly changing, we closely follow innovations, integrate advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence into business processes and offer solutions ahead of the age to our customers.

Our Values

Ajans Mobil continues to sign projects that make a difference in the industry with its commitment to ethical values, transparent and reliable service understanding. We enable our customers to create a strong presence in the digital world by analyzing their needs in the most correct way and offering them special solutions.

By working with Ajans Mobil, you can bring your brand together with the opportunities brought by the digital age and make a difference.
