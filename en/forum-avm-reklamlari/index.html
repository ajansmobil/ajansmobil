Forum Shopping Mall - Center of Shopping and Entertainment

Forum Shopping Mall holds an important place in Gaziantep's social and commercial life as one of the city's most prestigious and established shopping centers. With its central location, wide store variety and quality service approach, it hosts thousands of visitors daily. Our advertising spaces located in this shopping mall increase your brand's value and enable you to reach a wide customer base by reaching 200-500 thousand people weekly.

Forum Shopping Mall is the shopping center where international brands meet most intensively in Gaziantep. Along with world-renowned brands such as Zara, H&M, Mango, LC Waikiki, Teknosa, MediaMarkt, local premium brands are also located in Forum Shopping Mall. Advertising in this prestigious environment enables your brand to create a quality perception at international standards. Consumers automatically perceive brands they see in Forum Shopping Mall as reliable, quality and at global standards. This perception increases your brand value and customer trust.

Advertising options are offered in areas such as digital screens, billboards and parking. Digital screens are placed in central locations, enabling you to share dynamic content. Our digital screens positioned near main entrances, escalators, food court and cinema halls are seen by thousands of people daily. You can present your brand more impressively with video advertisements, animations and cyclical visuals.

Parking advertising areas are an ideal medium for vehicle owners. In Forum Shopping Mall's spacious parking area, we offer advertising spaces on columns, walls and directional panels. Vehicle owners see your advertisements while parking, returning to their vehicles and walking in the parking lot. Parking advertisements yield effective results especially for brands in automotive, insurance, finance and real estate sectors.

Forum Shopping Mall is an important socializing venue preferred by families, young couples and friend groups on weekends. Especially on Saturdays and Sundays, visitor numbers reach their peak. Families shop, eat and have fun with their children. Young people meet with friends, go to the cinema and spend time in cafes. This diverse demographic structure enables brands from every sector to reach their target audience.

Forum Shopping Mall's cinema halls show the city's most popular films and host thousands of moviegoers every week. Our advertising spaces located at cinema entrances and corridors attract the attention of cinema audiences. Cinema areas are strategically important especially for brands wanting to reach a young and dynamic audience. Advertisements shown before and during film intervals capture the high attention of viewers.

As Ajans Mobil, we offer comprehensive advertising solutions at Forum Shopping Mall. We have premium advertising options at strategic points such as main entrances, central corridors, food court, cinema areas, parking regions, elevators and escalators. Detailed visitor traffic analysis, demographic target audience profile, visibility duration and interaction performance metrics of each advertising location are professionally reported. We provide expert consulting services to help you choose the most suitable advertising point for your campaign budget and marketing goals. Take your place at Forum Shopping Mall and introduce your brand at Gaziantep's prestige center!
