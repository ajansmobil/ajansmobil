Public Bus Advertising - Premium Visibility on Special Routes

Private public buses are an important public transport vehicle operating alongside municipal buses and serving on special routes. As Ajans Mobil, we offer premium advertising solutions on private public buses in cooperation with Geveze Agency. These buses generally consist of more comfortable and modern vehicles and appeal to upper segment passenger profiles.

Private public bus advertisements reaching 300-600 thousand people weekly are highly effective in terms of brand prestige and visibility. Advertising especially on special routes operating between business centers, shopping malls and university campuses enables you to reach working people and student groups directly. This advertising medium is ideal for brands wanting to project a modern and urban image.

Our private public bus advertising services offered in cooperation with Ajans Mobil and Geveze Agency include full vehicle wrapping, half vehicle wrapping, interior advertisements and billboard options at bus stops. Buses operating on special routes generally cover longer distances and carry more passengers. This way, your advertisement is seen in a wider geography and by more people.
