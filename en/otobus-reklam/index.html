Gaziantep Bus Advertisements - Mass Brand Visibility

Bus advertisements are one of the most effective outdoor advertising tools that allow your brand to reach thousands of people simultaneously. As Ajans Mobil, we have been providing professional advertising services for municipal buses, public buses and metro vehicles in Gaziantep for 15 years. Thanks to our agreements with Gaziantep Metropolitan Municipality and private public transportation companies, we provide advertising spaces on the city's busiest routes. You have the opportunity to promote your brand to tens of thousands of passengers and pedestrian traffic daily.

In Gaziantep, an average of 500 thousand people use public transportation daily. With bus advertisements, you reach not only passengers inside vehicles but also everyone walking on streets, driving vehicles and waiting at stops. Your bus advertisement attracts thousands of people's attention especially at busy intersections, while waiting at red lights or in heavy traffic. We offer advertising opportunities on all routes going to Şahinbey, Şehitkamil, Organized Industrial Zone, University campus and city center.

Our bus advertising services include full vehicle wrapping, half wrapping, side panel advertisements, rear surface advertisements and bus interior advertising spaces. 12-meter buses' wide surfaces offer a perfect canvas for creative and impressive designs. We make your brand unforgettable with 3D effects, panoramic visuals and attention-grabbing slogans. We maintain your advertisements' quality throughout all seasons by using UV-protective, waterproof and weather-resistant professional foil materials.

Bus advertisements are ideal not only for large brands but also for small and medium-sized businesses. They can be used for campaign launches, new product promotions, seasonal discounts, opening announcements and corporate image studies. Educational institutions, health organizations, retail chains, automotive dealers, construction companies and businesses in the service sector get high efficiency from bus advertisements.

Our campaign process consists of route analysis, design work, printing and production, installation and follow-up stages. We determine the routes where you will best reach your target audience. Our professional design team prepares message-focused and visually strong designs suitable for your brand identity. We guarantee the vibrancy and clarity of colors with high-resolution digital printing technology. Our installation team performs flawless application on the vehicle surface without air bubbles and wrinkles. During the campaign period, we monitor the advertisement's status with periodic checks and provide maintenance and repair services when necessary. We offer the most strategic advertising spaces to our customers with our 15 years of experience and strong relationships in Gaziantep public transportation sector. Contact us immediately to multiply your brand awareness in a short time with bus advertisements.
