Gaziantep Taxi Advertisements - Premium Brand Visibility

Taxi advertisements are a premium advertising medium that reaches your brand at every point of the city, especially to A+ income groups and business world. As Ajans Mobil, we have been providing illuminated taxi top signs, taxi wrapping and in-taxi advertising services in Gaziantep for 15 years. We organize advertising campaigns in a wide taxi fleet thanks to our agreements with taxi stands and individual taxi owners operating in Gaziantep. Your brand stays mobile 24/7 with taxis traveling hundreds of kilometers daily, going to every neighborhood of the city.

The demographic profile of taxi users is usually business people, managers and professionals in decision-making positions belonging to middle-high income groups. Therefore, taxi advertisements are very effective especially for B2B services, premium brands, financial services, insurance companies, private hospitals, high-value products and luxury service providers. You reach your target audience directly with taxis frequently going to premium points such as airports, hotels, business centers, hospitals and city centers.

Illuminated taxi top signs (taxi top) are an eye-catching and effective advertising space visible day and night. Your brand is brightly visible even in the dark with our double-sided LED illuminated signs. Our signs produced in 70x40 cm and 80x50 cm sizes with high-resolution printing technology are noticeable from a distance in traffic. Especially in the evening hours, your illuminated advertisement on taxis circulating in the city center reaches thousands of people.

With our taxi wrapping (full/half wrapping) service, we cover the entire taxi or a part of the taxi body with your brand's colors, logo and message. It is especially preferred during campaign periods, product launches or long-term brand awareness studies. We apply using high-quality vehicle foil materials without damaging the vehicle's original paint. We also provide foil removal and cleaning service after the campaign.

We reach passengers directly with in-taxi advertisements. We present your brand to taxi passengers' attention with front seat back tablet screens, seat back brochure pockets and headrest advertisements. You can integrate with your digital campaigns by adding QR codes and get instantly measurable results. An average taxi in Gaziantep carries 15-20 passengers daily and travels 150-200 km. On a monthly basis, a taxi reaches thousands of people and is seen by tens of thousands. With fleet campaigns, you can achieve dominant visibility citywide by advertising on 10, 20, 50 or more taxis simultaneously. With our 15 years of experience and strong connections in the taxi sector, we organize advertising campaigns in the highest quality taxi fleet at the most affordable prices. Contact us immediately for premium brand visibility.
