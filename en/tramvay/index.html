Gaziantep Tramway Advertising - Visibility in Modern Public Transport

Tramway advertising is a prestigious advertising medium that introduces your brand to thousands of passengers and city residents in modern urban transportation systems. As Ajans Mobil, we have been providing tramway vehicle wrapping, tramway station advertising and tramway interior advertising services in Gaziantep for 15 years. We have strategic advertising spaces on tramway lines operated by Gaziantep Metropolitan Municipality, at modern stations and inside vehicles. We maximize your brand visibility with tramways used by tens of thousands of people daily, passing through the city's most central routes.

Gaziantep tramway system serves on a strategic line reaching from the city center to the Organized Industrial Zone, university campus and residential areas. The tramway, which is intensively used by students, workers and city residents especially during morning and evening hours, offers the opportunity to reach a wide demographic from all ages and income groups. Tramway users are generally regular passengers; this enables your brand to reach the same people repeatedly and increases brand memorability.

We create a giant moving billboard by covering the exterior surfaces of tramway vehicles with your brand's visual identity with our tramway wrapping (full/half wrapping) service. 30-40 meter long tramway vehicles are seen by thousands of people while passing through the city's busiest streets. You stand out from your competitors and create a memorable brand image with panoramic designs, creative concepts and 3D effects. We produce long-lasting advertisements using high-quality, UV-protective and weather-resistant tramway foil materials.

We attract full attention to your brand during passengers' waiting moments with tramway station advertising. We organize multi-faceted advertising campaigns with digital LED screens, illuminated posters, wall graphics and ceiling-hung banners located at station platforms. Average waiting time at stations is 5-10 minutes; during this time, passengers examine the environment and show high attention to advertisements. Additionally, stations' clean, modern and aesthetic environment ensures your brand is remembered with a prestigious image.

We deliver messages directly to passengers with tramway interior advertising. There are numerous advertising points inside tramways with wall posters, ceiling-hung cards, seat-top advertisements and digital screens. Passengers stay in tramways for an average of 20-30 minutes and show intense interest in interior advertisements during this time. Educational institutions, technology brands, retail chains, health services and financial institutions get high returns from tramway advertising. Tramway advertising especially enables you to reach a young, educated, urban and environmentally conscious audience. Our campaign packages include 1-month, 3-month, 6-month and annual options. We offer design, printing, installation, municipal permits and periodic maintenance services as a complete package. We provide the most advantageous solutions in tramway advertising thanks to our 15 years of experience and strong relationships with Gaziantep Metropolitan Municipality. Contact us immediately to make your brand visible in modern public transport.
