Disclosure Text on Protection and Processing of Personal Data

In accordance with Law No. 6698 on Protection of Personal Data (KVKK), we would like to inform you about the processing of your personal data as Ajans Mobil Gaziantep in the capacity of data controller. This disclosure text contains detailed information about the purposes for which your personal data will be processed, legal reasons for processing, to whom and for what purposes it may be transferred, data collection method, retention periods and your rights.

Data Controller

Data Controller: Ajans Mobil Gaziantep

Address: Gaziantep, Turkey

E-mail: info@ajansmobil.com

Phone: 0531 679 10 14

Website: www.ajansmobil.com

Processed Personal Data Categories

Personal data categories processed by us are as follows: Identity Information: Name, surname, Turkish ID number (when required), date of birth. Contact Information: E-mail address, phone number, address information. Customer Transaction Information: Service request information, offer and contract information, project details, communication history. Financial Information: Bank account information, invoice information, payment information. Marketing Information: Cookie records, marketing permissions, campaign preferences. Legal Transaction and Compliance Information: Lawsuit and execution tracking information, legal compliance information. Transaction Security Information: IP address, log records, security camera records (in office visits).

Processing Purposes and Legal Reasons of Personal Data

Your personal data is processed for the following purposes and legal reasons: Service Provision: Provision of web design, digital marketing, SEO, social media management and outdoor advertising services (Establishment and performance of contract). Customer Relationship Management: Communication, request and complaint management, customer satisfaction measurement (Legitimate interest). Financial and Accounting Transactions: Invoicing, payment tracking, accounting records (Legal obligation). Marketing and Advertising Activities: Campaign notifications, newsletters, special offers (Explicit consent). Business Development: Market research, service development, business analytics (Legitimate interest). Legal Compliance: Fulfillment of legal obligations, management of legal disputes (Legal obligation). Security: Information security, fraud prevention, prevention of unauthorized access (Legitimate interest).

Transfer of Personal Data

Your personal data may be transferred to our business partners (hosting providers, payment institutions, e-invoice systems), public institutions and organizations within the framework of legal obligations, our legal consultants and audit firms in order to achieve the purposes stated above. In case of data transfer abroad, provisions of Article 9 of KVKK will be complied with.

Retention Period of Personal Data

Your personal data is retained for the period required by processing purposes and as long as retention periods foreseen in relevant legislation. Financial records are retained for 10 years in accordance with Tax Procedure Law, commercial records for 10 years in accordance with Turkish Commercial Code, employment contract and payroll information for 10 years in accordance with Labor Law. After these periods expire, data is deleted, destroyed or anonymized.

Data Subject Rights

As a data subject within the scope of Article 11 of KVKK, your rights are as follows: Learning whether your personal data is processed, requesting information if processed, learning the processing purpose and whether it is used in accordance with the purpose, knowing third parties to whom it is transferred domestically or abroad, requesting correction if incompletely or incorrectly processed, requesting deletion or destruction if reasons requiring processing disappear, requesting notification of correction, deletion and destruction operations to third parties to whom it is transferred, objecting to results arising against you due to analysis exclusively by automated systems, requesting compensation for damage if you suffer damage due to unlawful processing. To exercise these rights, you can send an e-mail to info@ajansmobil.com together with documents identifying your identity or make a written application through a notary. Your applications will be evaluated within 30 days at the latest.
