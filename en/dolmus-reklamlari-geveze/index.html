Shared Taxi Advertising (Geveze) - Flexible and Economic Advertising Solutions

Shared taxi advertisements offered in cooperation with Geveze Agency are a special advertising medium reaching Gaziantep's neighborhood areas and non-central regions. As Ajans Mobil, we carry your brand to every corner of the city by utilizing Geveze Agency's extensive shared taxi fleet. Shared taxi vehicles enter narrow streets and neighborhood interiors that municipal buses cannot reach, enabling you to reach your target audience more closely.

The most important advantage of shared taxi advertisements is flexible route options and economic pricing. This advertising medium reaching 300-600 thousand people weekly is ideal especially for local businesses, neighborhood tradesmen and regional brands. Shared taxis work from early morning hours until late evening hours, making your brand visible at all hours of the day.

Our shared taxi advertising services offered in cooperation with Ajans Mobil and Geveze Agency include vehicle wrapping, side panel advertisements and interior space advertising options. Advertising especially on shared taxis operating on neighborhood routes enables you to reach local customers directly. This advertising medium is a strategic investment for your goals of building brand awareness and increasing local market share.
