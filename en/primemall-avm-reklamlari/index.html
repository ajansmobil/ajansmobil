Primemall - Gaziantep's Prestige Center

Primemall is one of Gaziantep's largest and most prestigious shopping centers. Primemall, which hosts thousands of visitors daily on weekends and weekdays, is an important center where the pulse of the city's social and commercial life beats. Our advertising spaces located in this shopping mall enable you to promote your brand most effectively by reaching 200-500 thousand people weekly. We make your brand visible from every corner with LED screen, billboard and interior space advertising options.

Primemall is not just a shopping center; it is also the center of entertainment, dining and social activities. It is a living space that brings together people of all ages and segments with cinema halls, restaurants, cafes, play areas and event spaces. Families prefer Primemall for weekend outings, young people to spend time with friends, and business people for meals and meetings. This diverse demographic structure enables brands from every sector to reach their target audience.

Our advertising spaces within the shopping mall are positioned at points with heavy traffic such as main entrances, central corridors, escalators, elevators and food court. Visitors see your advertisement repeatedly during their time at the shopping mall. Your brand is constantly in front of their eyes while shopping, eating, waiting for cinema or going to event areas. This constant exposure provides great advantages in terms of brand awareness and purchase decisions.

Our LED screen advertising options enable you to share dynamic content. You can present your brand more impressively with video advertisements, animations and cyclical visuals. LED screens are an ideal option especially for campaign announcements, new product launches and seasonal promotions. You can update your content instantly and give different messages at different hours.

One of Primemall's most important advantages is its location adjacent to Iconova Street. Thanks to this strategic proximity, you can maximize your brand's visibility by benefiting from both advertising spaces within the shopping mall and on the street together. 80% of visitors coming to Primemall also spend time on Iconova Street. Running a combined advertising campaign at these two locations increases your brand's memorability by up to 300% and directly affects purchase decisions.

Shopping malls, which are the stop point for people with all kinds of consumption habits, give you a prestigious brand impression in addition to gaining new customers. Primemall advertising spaces are strategically important especially for brands in fashion, cosmetics, technology, food, entertainment and finance sectors. Visitors are open to discovering new products and services while wandering in the shopping mall and their purchase tendencies are high.

As Ajans Mobil, we offer comprehensive advertising solutions at Primemall. We have premium advertising options at strategic points such as main entrances, central squares, food court, cinema entrances, parking areas and escalators. Detailed visitor traffic analysis, demographic target audience profile, visibility duration and interaction performance metrics of each advertising location are professionally reported. We provide expert consulting services to help you choose the most suitable advertising point for your campaign budget and marketing goals. Take your place at Primemall and introduce your brand at Gaziantep's prestige center!
