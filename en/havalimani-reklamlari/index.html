Gaziantep Airport - Prestige and Accessibility

Gaziantep Airport is one of the city's most important transportation centers and serves millions of passengers annually. The airport with intensive domestic and international flights is a critical stop point for business people, tourists and guests coming from outside the city. Our advertising spaces located in this region enable you to introduce your brand in a prestigious way by reaching 50-100 thousand people weekly.

Airport advertisements offer ideal opportunities for your target audience to see your brand during pre-travel, travel and post-travel processes. We have advertising spaces at strategic points such as check-in areas, waiting lounges, baggage claim areas and exit gates. Passengers see your advertisements repeatedly during their time at the airport and your brand is etched into their memories.

Airport user profile generally consists of middle and upper segment income groups. Business people, managers, entrepreneurs and families traveling for vacation form the main target audience of your airport advertisements. This audience exhibits a profile with high purchasing power, brand conscious and open to new products/services. Introducing your brand to this valuable target audience offers great opportunities for long-term customer acquisition.

Our advertisements prepared with digital printing technology provide long-lasting and impressive appearance by offering high-quality visuals. In the airport's modern and clean environment, professional-looking advertisements increase your brand's prestige. You can share dynamic content with LED screen and digital advertising options and update your campaigns instantly.

Airport advertisements offer an ideal advertising medium especially for hotels, restaurants, car rental companies, technology brands, banks and insurance companies. Passengers research services they will need at their destinations at the airport. Seeing your brand at this critical moment can influence their preferences and provide customer acquisition.

Gaziantep Airport serves not only locally but also passengers coming from major cities such as Istanbul, Ankara, Izmir and from abroad. This way, you catch the opportunity to introduce your brand at regional and national levels. Airport advertisements are strategically important especially for brands operating in tourism, accommodation and service sectors.

As Ajans Mobil, we offer comprehensive advertising solutions at Gaziantep Airport. We have premium advertising spaces at strategic points such as check-in areas, VIP waiting lounges, baggage claim regions, exit gates and parking entrances. We share detailed passenger traffic analysis, demographic target audience profile and visibility performance report of each advertising location with our customers. Advertise at the airport and move your brand to a prestigious and accessible position!
