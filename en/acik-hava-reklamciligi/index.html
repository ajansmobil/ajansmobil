Outdoor Advertising - Be Visible at Gaziantep's Strategic Points

Outdoor Advertising is a classic and effective marketing method that makes your brand visible at the most strategic points of the city, at locations with the heaviest traffic. As Ajans Mobil, we have been providing professional services in billboards, totems, megalights, digital LED screens, facade wrapping, bridge banners, stop advertisements and street furniture advertisements in Gaziantep for 15 years. We have premium advertising spaces at the city's busiest intersections, main artery roads, shopping mall entrances and central business districts.

Billboard advertisements are the most classic and effective form of outdoor advertising. Our billboard spaces in 6x3 meters, 8x4 meters and special sizes are positioned at points with the highest traffic density in Gaziantep. We have strategic billboard locations at bus station intersection, Karataş intersection, Organized Industrial Zone entrance, 100th Year Boulevard, Kilis road and city entrances/exits. We offer the opportunity to introduce your brand to hundreds of thousands of vehicle and pedestrian traffic daily.

Totem advertisements are especially ideal for retail sector, automotive dealers, gas stations, restaurants and service businesses. We make your brand visible from a distance with totem advertisements rising in front of or near your business, illuminated at night. We create a prestigious corporate identity with 3D letters, LED lighting and modern designs. We maximize your night visibility by offering two-way, illuminated advertising spaces with megalight systems.

Digital LED screen advertisements are today's most effective and dynamic outdoor advertising solution. We attract viewers' attention 80% more with moving visuals, video content and real-time messages. We have a digital screen network at Gaziantep's busiest points. You can start your campaign instantly, change content whenever you want, give different messages at different hours of the day. It is a perfect medium for special days, discount campaigns and instant announcements.

We transform building facades into giant advertising spaces with facade wrapping and banner advertisements. They are used for construction companies, new project launches, large campaigns and event sponsorships. We introduce your brand to hundreds of people waiting at bus stops with stop advertisements. We reach pedestrian traffic in the city center with street furniture advertisements (sidewalk signs, bench advertisements). We also have alternative outdoor advertising solutions such as bridge banners, entrance-exit banners and directional signs. Our outdoor advertising services include location research, municipal permits, design and production, installation and maintenance processes. With our 15 years of experience, strong relationships with Gaziantep Metropolitan Municipality and advertising rights at premium locations, we bring the most effective outdoor advertising campaigns to life. Contact us immediately to make your brand visible at every point of Gaziantep.
