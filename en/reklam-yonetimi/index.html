Professional Advertising Management - Google Ads & Social Media Ads

As Ajans Mobil, we have been providing professional digital advertising management services in Gaziantep for 15 years. We increase your business's sales and brand awareness with our expert team in Google Ads (Google AdWords), Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, LinkedIn Ads and YouTube advertisements. Our digital marketing experts with Google Partner certification ensure your advertising budget is used most efficiently by designing ROI (Return on Investment) focused campaigns.

Within the scope of our Google Ads management service; we organize search network advertisements (Search Ads), display network (Display Network), shopping advertisements (Shopping Ads), video advertisements (YouTube Ads) and remarketing campaigns. We determine the most searched and conversion-providing words in your industry by doing detailed keyword research. We increase click-through rates (CTR) and decrease cost/click (CPC) rates by optimizing your advertisement texts with A/B testing method.

In social media advertising management, we reach users with the most accurate demographic characteristics by doing target audience analysis on Facebook and Instagram platforms. We use your advertising budget most efficiently with age, gender, location, interests, behaviors and lookalike (similar audiences) targeting. We design creative campaigns with different format options such as catalog advertisements, story advertisements, carousel (rotating gallery) advertisements and video advertisements.

We measure which advertisements bring valuable actions such as sales, form filling or phone calls by setting up conversion tracking in every advertising campaign. We analyze user behaviors on your website with Google Analytics integration, determining which traffic sources provide the best conversion. We share your campaign performance transparently by preparing monthly detailed reports.

We organize special Google Shopping advertisements management for e-commerce businesses, Google My Business optimization and map advertisements for local businesses, lead generation (potential customer collection) campaigns for service sector. We can increase your conversion rates up to 300% by reaching users who visit your website but do not make purchases again with remarketing strategies. With our 15 years of experience and hundreds of successful campaign references, we are your reliable solution partner in digital advertising management in Gaziantep. Contact us immediately to use your advertising budget most efficiently and increase your sales.
