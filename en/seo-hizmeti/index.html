SEO Service - Rank High on Google

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) are strategic works that enable your website to rank high on Google, Yandex and other search engines. As Ajans Mobil, we have been providing professional SEO services in Gaziantep for 15 years, increasing the organic traffic and sales of hundreds of businesses. We provide permanent and sustainable digital growth while reducing advertising expenses.

Our SEO work begins with a detailed site analysis. We check technical elements of your website such as speed, mobile compatibility, security (SSL), sitemap, robots.txt file and structured data by performing technical SEO audit. We continuously monitor your site health and performance metrics with Google Search Console and Google Analytics integrations. We apply advanced SEO optimizations such as broken link detection, canonical tags, hreflang tag and schema markup.

Keyword research is the cornerstone of our SEO work. We identify keywords specific to your sector, with high search volume and low competition. We increase your conversion rates by reaching niche target audiences with long-tail keywords. By performing competitor analysis, we examine SEO strategies of prominent websites in your sector and develop plans to achieve better results.

We increase your website's value with our content optimization and content production services. We prepare SEO-compliant blog posts, category page contents and product descriptions. Each content is produced to use the target keyword naturally, prioritize user experience and comply with Google's E-A-T (Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) criteria. We pay attention to details such as image optimization, alt text tags and heading hierarchy.

Within the scope of our off-page SEO work, we perform quality backlink work. We enable you to gain natural and reliable links from high-authority websites related to your sector. We perform local SEO work with Google My Business optimization, ensuring your business appears in top ranks in map searches in Gaziantep. We increase your visibility in the local market with customer review management, NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency and local directory registrations. We share your keyword rankings, organic traffic increase and conversion metrics with monthly SEO reports. With our 15 years of SEO experience, we guarantee long-term and safe results using white hat SEO techniques compatible with Google's constantly changing algorithm.
