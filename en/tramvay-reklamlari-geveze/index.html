Tramway Advertising (Geveze) - Premium Visibility in Rail System

Gaziantep tramway system is the heart of the city's modern rail transport network and serves tens of thousands of passengers daily. As Ajans Mobil, we offer tramway wrapping, station platform advertisements, vehicle interior advertisements and digital screen options at stops in cooperation with Geveze Agency. Since tramways operate on fixed routes, your advertising visibility is guaranteed and your target audience sees your advertisement regularly.

Tramway advertisements reaching 300-600 thousand people weekly are an ideal medium especially for the 18-45 age group of young and working segments. Tramway users generally exhibit an educated and high purchasing power profile such as university students, office workers, teachers and healthcare personnel. This demographic structure is strategically important for brands in technology, education, finance and service sectors.

Our tramway advertising services offered in cooperation with Ajans Mobil and Geveze Agency include billboards at station platforms, vehicle interior ceiling and side panel advertisements, digital screens at stops and full vehicle wrapping options. Advertising especially at university, hospital and business center stations enables you to reach your target audience directly. Tramway advertisements are ideal for brands wanting to project a modern, environmentally friendly and urban image.
