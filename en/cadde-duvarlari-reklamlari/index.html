Street Walls - Your Brand on the City's Walls

Street walls are advertising spaces located at the busiest points of the city and provide high visibility. These giant advertising spaces positioned on main streets, intersections and areas with heavy pedestrian traffic enable your brand to reach 200-500 thousand people weekly. Street walls offer a larger surface area than billboards, allowing your brand to be displayed in a more impressive and magnificent way.

Our wall advertisements on Gaziantep's busiest streets provide maximum visibility from both pedestrian and vehicle traffic. Thousands of people going to work in the morning, going out during lunch breaks, and returning home in the evening walk on these streets every day and see your brand. People who regularly use the same route see your advertisement repeatedly and your brand is etched into their memories. This constant exposure provides great advantages in terms of brand awareness and memorability.

Our advertisements prepared with digital printing technology provide long-lasting and impressive appearance by offering high-quality visuals. Colorful, vibrant and large-sized visuals attract attention even from a distance and arouse curiosity. Especially creative and aesthetic designs cause people to take photos and share them on social media. This organic spread multiplies your brand's reach.

Street walls are ideal for long-term advertising campaigns. A wall advertisement mounted once remains durable for years and requires minimum maintenance. This also offers a very economical option in terms of cost per advertisement. It is a strategic investment especially for brands aiming to build brand awareness and increase long-term market share.

Street wall advertisements yield effective results for brands in fashion, food, technology, education, finance and service sectors. The potential to reach wide target audiences makes brands from every sector prefer this medium. It is an ideal platform especially for new product launches, campaign announcements and brand image studies.

As Ajans Mobil, we offer premium wall advertising spaces on Gaziantep's most strategic streets. We have distinguished locations on main arteries such as İnönü Street, Suburcu Street, İncilipınar Street, Atatürk Boulevard and at central intersections. Detailed traffic analysis, daily pedestrian and vehicle counts, demographic target audience profile and visibility performance metrics of each location are professionally reported. We provide expert consulting services to help you choose the most suitable wall advertising point for your campaign budget and marketing goals.

Street wall advertisements are visible 24/7. Colors shining with sunlight during the day, your brand attracts attention at all hours with street lighting in the evening. Even during night hours, your advertisement remains visible thanks to street lighting. This uninterrupted visibility ensures your brand is etched into memories. Take your place on the streets' walls with Ajans Mobil and make your brand a symbol of the city!
