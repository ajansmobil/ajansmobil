Building Elevators - Close-Range Advertising

Advertising in building elevators is one of the most effective ways to reach your target audience from the closest distance. People spend an average of 30-60 seconds in elevators during their daily routines, and during this time they actively see and read advertisements because they cannot do anything else. This close distance and intense attention ensures that your brand message is etched into memories. It enables you to promote your brand most effectively by reaching 100-400 thousand people weekly.

Advertising serves as a tool to bring you closer to your goals. Companies that do not advertise generally employ marketing staff, and these staff must be sufficient in number. Elevator advertising offers a more affordable and effective solution than personnel costs. At the same time, it provides a much less intrusive and more professional approach than door-to-door marketing methods.

Elevator advertising yields effective results especially in residential complexes, business centers and office buildings. People who use elevators every morning going to work, during lunch breaks and returning home in the evening see your advertisement several times a day. This regular exposure provides great advantages in terms of brand awareness and memorability. It is an ideal medium especially for local service providers, restaurants, dry cleaners, hairdressers, gyms and supermarket chains.

Our advertisements prepared with digital printing technology provide long-lasting and impressive appearance by offering high-quality visuals. In the clean and enclosed environment of elevators, advertisements remain for a long time without wear and fading. This also offers a very economical option in terms of cost per advertisement. Additionally, advertising in elevators helps your brand project a modern and urban image.

One of the greatest advantages of elevator advertising is target audience segmentation. By choosing which region and which type of buildings to advertise in, you can reach your target audience more precisely. For example, you can reach upper segment consumers in luxury residential complexes, white-collar workers in business centers, and young audiences in student dormitories. This segmentation allows you to use your advertising budget most efficiently.

As Ajans Mobil, we offer professional advertising spaces in hundreds of elevators in different regions of Gaziantep. We have a wide advertising network in different segments such as central locations, luxury residential projects, business centers, shopping malls and student dormitories. Detailed building profile, daily user count, demographic target audience information and advertising performance metrics of each elevator point are professionally reported. We provide expert consulting services to help you choose the most suitable elevator advertising points for your campaign budget and target audience.

Elevator advertising enables you to increase interaction by supporting your campaign messages with QR codes, discount coupons or web addresses. People can scan QR codes while waiting in elevators, note discount coupons or visit web addresses. This interaction provides direct conversion and helps you measure the return on your advertising investment. Take your place in building elevators with Ajans Mobil and reach your target audience from the closest distance!
