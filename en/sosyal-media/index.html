Social Media Management - Grow Your Brand Digitally

Social media is the channel where brands communicate most directly and effectively with their customers today. As Ajans Mobil, we have been providing professional social media management services in Gaziantep for 15 years. We strengthen your brand's digital presence on Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok and YouTube platforms, increasing your follower count and engagement rates. Our experienced team is at your service to create social media strategies, prepare content calendars, design creative visuals and manage communities.

Our social media management service starts with a detailed brand analysis. We determine a social media strategy specific to you by analyzing your target audience, competitors and industry trends. We plan what topics your brand will produce content on by creating content pillars. We share different content types such as educational content, entertaining content, user-generated content (UGC), product promotions and campaign announcements in a balanced way.

Our graphic design team prepares eye-catching and professional visuals suitable for your brand's visual identity. We produce content in different formats such as Instagram feed visuals, story designs, carousel posts, reel videos and infographics. With our video production service, we shoot product promotion videos, customer testimonial videos and brand story videos. We visualize your products and services in the best way with our professional photography service.

Within the scope of our community management service, we track comments, messages and tags coming to your accounts, providing fast and professional responses. We resolve customer complaints, thank positive comments and build sincere relationships with your followers. We are experienced in crisis management; we manage negative situations with correct communication strategies.

We promote your brand to wide audiences by organizing influencer marketing campaigns. We make collaboration agreements with micro and macro influencers suitable for your industry, organizing product promotions and brand collaborations. We increase your organic reach with social media advertising campaigns, enabling you to reach your target audience faster. We provide follower acquisition, website traffic increase and sales conversion with Instagram Ads, Facebook Ads and TikTok Ads management. We share follower growth, engagement rates, reach metrics and content performance by preparing monthly analytical reports. With our 15 years of experience, we help you create a strong brand presence on social media.
