Gaziantep Shared Taxi Advertisements - Be Visible Everywhere in the City

Shared taxi advertisements are one of the most effective outdoor advertising solutions that make your brand visible in every neighborhood and every street of Gaziantep. As Ajans Mobil, we have been providing professional services in shared taxi and minibus advertisements for 15 years. We have a wide advertising network in city public transport vehicles. Maximize your brand awareness with your mobile advertisements seen by thousands of people daily.

The biggest advantage of shared taxi advertisements is that you can reach your target audience continuously and repeatedly. Your advertisement becomes visible 24/7 on shared taxis circulating on Gaziantep's busiest routes from early morning to late evening. We have advertising opportunities in central areas such as Şahinbey, Şehitkamil, İbrahimli, İncilipınar, Güneykent, Karataş and all neighborhood lines.

Our shared taxi advertising services include full vehicle wrapping, half vehicle wrapping, side panel advertisements and rear window advertisements. Our professional design team prepares designs suitable for your brand's visual identity, readable from a distance, eye-catching and aesthetic. We ensure your advertisements stay vibrant and effective for a long time by using high-quality digital printing technology and durable advertising foil materials.

Shared taxi advertisements are very effective especially for local businesses, retail stores, restaurants and cafes, educational institutions, health centers, beauty salons and real estate offices. Since more than 70% of Gaziantep's population uses shared taxis for daily transportation, your brand reaches a wide demography. You have the opportunity to convey your message to young, middle-aged people from all income groups.

Our shared taxi advertising campaigns include design, printing, installation, agreement with driver and periodic controls. We offer solutions suitable for your budget with monthly, 3-month, 6-month and annual package options. We position your advertisements on routes where your target audience uses intensively by doing route analysis. We can report the daily distance traveled and locations passed by your vehicles with GPS tracking system. Shared taxi advertisements, which are much more economical than billboard, TV or radio advertisements, are one of the most efficient advertising channels in terms of cost/reach ratio. With our 15 years of experience and wide shared taxi fleet in Gaziantep, we make your brand visible at every point of the city. Contact us immediately for mobile advertising solutions.
