User Rights and Responsibilities

All users who use Ajans Mobil website, benefit from our services or contact us have certain rights and responsibilities. This page explains your user rights, website terms of use, copyright information and your responsibilities. By using our website, you are deemed to have accepted these terms.

User Rights

As users using our website, you have the following rights: Right to Information: Right to receive accurate and up-to-date information about services we offer, pricing, company information and communication channels. Service Quality: Right to receive professional, timely and contract-compliant service. Protection of Personal Data: Protection of your personal data within the scope of KVKK, learning processing purposes and exercising your rights. Complaint and Objection: Right to convey complaints, suggestions and objections regarding our services and receive response. Cancellation and Refund: Right to request service cancellation and refund within contract terms. Secure Communication: Right to share your personal and financial information through secure channels. Equal Treatment: Right to receive fair and equal service without discrimination.

User Responsibilities

Responsibilities of users using our website are as follows: Providing Accurate Information: Responsibility to provide accurate, complete and up-to-date information in contact forms, offer requests and contract processes. Legal Compliance: Responsibility to use the website in accordance with laws, moral rules and rights of third parties. Respect for Intellectual Property: Responsibility to respect copyrights of contents on the website and not use without permission. Security: Responsibility to keep your account information (if any) secure and take precautions against unauthorized use. Not Causing Harm: Responsibility not to engage in activities that will harm website operation, hack the system or spread malicious software. Commercial Use: Responsibility not to engage in commercial data collection, sending spam or activities that will violate competition laws.
