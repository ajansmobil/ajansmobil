M1 Shopping Mall - Where Modern Architecture and Wide Spaces Meet

M1 Shopping Mall is one of Gaziantep's most attention-grabbing shopping centers with its modern architecture and wide space. With its contemporary design, spacious interiors and wide store variety, it appeals to visitors of all ages and segments. Our advertising spaces located in this shopping mall enable your brand to attract attention and be memorable by reaching 200-500 thousand people weekly. We make your brand visible from every angle with advertising options we offer in interior and exterior spaces.

M1 Shopping Mall offers continuous access to your target audience as a center where social events are intense. It hosts many organizations such as concerts, exhibitions, children's events, fashion shows and product launches. These events attract extra visitors to the shopping mall and enable your brand to reach wider audiences. Especially on event days, your advertising spaces are seen by thousands of people and your brand takes place in memories.

M1 Shopping Mall, which attracts attention with its modern architecture, offers visitors a comfortable shopping experience with its wide corridors, high ceilings and atriums receiving natural light. Our advertising spaces located in this spacious and bright environment become visually more impressive and attention-grabbing. Especially hanging banners and digital screens in the atrium area, positioned at the center of the shopping mall, provide maximum visibility.

Our interior space advertising options include digital screens, billboards, elevator interior advertising spaces, restroom entrance advertising panels and corridor advertisements. Digital screens are positioned near main entrances, escalators, food court and event areas, enabling you to share dynamic content. You can present your brand more impressively with video advertisements, animations and cyclical visuals.

Our exterior space advertising options include the shopping mall's facade billboards, parking entrance advertising panels and parking area advertisements. M1 Shopping Mall's location on the main street enables exterior facade advertisements to be seen by thousands of vehicles and pedestrians daily. Especially illuminated billboards in the evening hours attract attention even from a distance and make your brand a symbol of the city.

M1 Shopping Mall is also famous for its food court and cafe variety. It offers a wide range of options from fast food restaurants to luxury restaurants, from cafeterias to cafes. The food court sees intense visitor traffic especially during lunch and evening hours. Families, business people, students and young groups meet at the food court and spend time. Our advertising spaces located in this region enable you to reach eating and socializing audiences directly.

M1 Shopping Mall is a center that sees continuous visitor traffic on weekends and weekdays. Especially on Saturdays and Sundays, families, young couples and friend groups shop, eat, go to the cinema and participate in events at the shopping mall. This intense visitor traffic ensures your brand is constantly visible and reaches wide audiences. It offers an ideal advertising medium for brands from every sector.

As Ajans Mobil, we offer comprehensive advertising solutions at M1 Shopping Mall. We have premium advertising options at strategic points such as main entrances, central corridors, atrium area, food court, parking areas, exterior facade, elevators and escalators. Detailed visitor traffic analysis, demographic target audience profile, visibility duration and interaction performance metrics of each advertising location are professionally reported. We provide expert consulting services to help you choose the most suitable advertising point for your campaign budget and marketing goals. Take your place at M1 Shopping Mall and introduce your brand at the point where modern architecture and wide spaces meet!
