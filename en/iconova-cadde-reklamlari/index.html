Iconova Street - Gaziantep's Prestige Center

Iconova Street is one of Gaziantep's most prestigious shopping and social life centers. With its modern architecture, luxury stores, restaurants and entertainment venues, it is the meeting point of the city's upper segment consumers. Our advertising spaces located in this region enable you to promote your brand most effectively by reaching 150-300 thousand people weekly.

Iconova Street is not just a shopping center; it is also a living space where the pulse of social life beats. With cafes, restaurants, cinemas and outdoor event areas, it sees intense visitor traffic on weekends and weekdays. Especially in the evening hours, a young and dynamic audience spends time in this region. Displaying your brand at this prestigious location automatically creates a value and trust perception in your target audience's eyes.

Our advertisements prepared with digital printing technology provide long-lasting and impressive appearance by offering high-quality visuals. We highlight your brand with colorful, vibrant and attention-grabbing designs. Billboards are positioned at strategic points on the street, providing maximum visibility from both pedestrian and vehicle traffic.

Iconova Street is also adjacent to Primemall Shopping Mall. This way, you benefit from both pedestrian traffic on the street and visitors coming to the shopping mall. Advertising in this region where thousands of people pass daily rapidly increases your brand's awareness. It is an ideal advertising point especially for brands in fashion, technology, food and entertainment sectors.

Our advertising spaces are visible 24/7. Your brand attracts attention at all hours with natural light during the day and lighting systems in the evening. You can share dynamic content with LED screen and Digiboard options and update your campaigns instantly. This flexibility provides great advantages for seasonal campaigns and special days.

As Ajans Mobil, we offer professional advertising solutions at Iconova Street. We have premium advertising spaces at strategic points such as street entrance, central squares and near Primemall Shopping Mall. We provide detailed consulting services to help you choose the most efficient point suitable for your campaign budget and marketing goals. We share daily traffic analysis, demographic target audience profile and visibility performance report of each advertising location with our customers.

Iconova Street is not only an important meeting point for Gaziantep's local market but also for tourists, business people and investors coming from outside the city. Everyone visiting Gaziantep definitely sees Iconova Street and spends time in this region. Therefore, advertising here offers a unique opportunity to promote your brand not only locally but also at regional, national and even international levels. Take your place at Iconova Street with Ajans Mobil's professional team and move your brand to a prestigious position!
