Organized Industrial Zone - Heart of B2B Marketing

Gaziantep Organized Industrial Zone (OSB) is one of the city's most important production and trade centers. This region where intense business life continues with thousands of factories, workshops, offices and warehouses is a production base where tens of thousands of workers, contractors, suppliers, business people and logistics personnel pass daily. Our advertising spaces located in this region offer unique opportunities for your B2B marketing strategies by reaching 150-300 thousand people weekly.

OSB advertisements are an ideal medium for industrial products, machinery equipment, raw material suppliers, logistics companies, construction machinery, security systems and industrial service providers. The target audience in this region consists of factory owners, general managers, purchasing managers and business administrators who are in positions to make purchasing decisions. Your advertising message reaches decision makers directly.

Our billboards are strategically positioned on OSB's main arteries and intersections. At these points where intense vehicle traffic is seen at the start of morning shift and evening shift exit, your brand is seen by thousands of people daily. Business people who use the same route every day see your advertisement regularly and your brand is etched into their memories.

Our advertisements prepared with digital printing technology provide long-lasting and impressive appearance by offering high-quality visuals. We highlight your brand with colorful, vibrant and professional designs. In an industrial region like OSB, clean and modern-looking advertisements reflect your brand's quality and professionalism.

OSB advertisements are effective not only locally but also at regional and national levels. Gaziantep OSB is one of Turkey's largest organized industrial zones and is frequently visited by business people, suppliers and customers coming from different cities. This way, you catch the opportunity to introduce your brand to a wide geography and expand your B2B network.

Your target audience seeing your advertising messages during business hours directly affects purchasing decisions. A factory owner or purchasing manager can remember a machinery equipment advertisement they saw while going to work in the morning when researching suppliers during the day. This reminder effect increases the probability of your brand being preferred and creates commercial opportunities.

As Ajans Mobil, we offer comprehensive B2B advertising solutions at Gaziantep OSB. We have premium billboard and digital screen options at strategic points such as main entrance gates, central intersections, factory-dense regions, logistics centers and cafeteria areas. We share detailed vehicle traffic analysis, business world target audience profile and visibility performance report of each advertising location with our customers. Take your place at OSB to strengthen your B2B marketing strategy and join the leaders of your sector!
